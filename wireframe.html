AP build notes · CNT-02
  • Four artefact cards in a horizontal row. Each card has a distinct colour-coded type label: teal (survey), navy (CTO), warm gold (board), muted red (compliance).
  • Card expand interaction: use object states in AP, not timeline show/hide. Each card has a collapsed and expanded state.
  • Track card views with four boolean variables: signal_1_viewed through signal_4_viewed.
  • Next button locked until all four variables are true. Condition applied to Next button trigger.
  • Counter at bottom left updates as each card is opened. Driven by a numeric variable incrementing on each view.
  • This is the primary portfolio differentiation screen. Prioritise artefact card design quality above all else on this screen.
Screen 4 of 5 · BRN-01 · Branching scenario · Est. 3 min
Day 7: Your First Strategic Call
Three-option decision layout. Order: Beginner — Conservative — Fashionista. Single selection. No change after confirm.
1280 × 720px · BRN-01
Scenario prompt
Sandra has asked for your initial read of where Meridian sits. You have reviewed the four signals and spoken informally with three senior leaders. Based on what you have seen, how would you classify Meridian's current position?
Option 01
Beginner
Limited capability and coordination across both dimensions. Starting from a low base in technology and leadership readiness.
Option 02
Conservative
Strong governance and control structures. Low technical agility. Stability at the cost of progress.
Option 03
Fashionista
Strong appetite for experimentation. Weak coordination and scaling capability. Energy without architecture.
Select one option to continue
Confirm — locked until selection made
AP build notes · BRN-01
  • Three choice cards in equal columns. Order left to right: Beginner, Conservative, Fashionista. Reflects ascending sophistication without signalling correct answer.
  • All three cards use identical visual styling. No colour or size hierarchy between options.
  • On selection: highlight selected card with navy border (1.5px). Confirm button unlocks.
  • On confirm: set variable diagnosis_choice = C (Fashionista), B (Conservative), or A (Beginner). Lock all options. Navigate to CNT-03.
  • Transformation Momentum: +1 for Fashionista, -1 for Conservative or Beginner.
  • No back navigation from this point. Decision is final once confirmed.
  • Choice title uses Cormorant Garamond 500. All other choice text uses DM Sans 300.
