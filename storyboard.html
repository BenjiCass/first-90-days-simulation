LX Storyboard: The First 90 Days: An AI Leadership Simulation

Module 1 of 1 — v1.0 — 27 March 2026

Audience
MBA Year 2 students with 5 to 8 years professional experience, preparing for senior leadership roles in AI-driven organisations
Duration
25 to 35 minutes
Authoring tool
ActivePresenter
Learning objectives
  1. By the end of this module, learners will be able to:
  2. Diagnose an organisation's AI readiness using the Digital Mastery framework
  3. Evaluate a GenAI investment proposal using a structured four-cost model
  4. Design a governance structure that balances transformation speed with organisational risk
  5. Make and defend strategic decisions under conditions of ambiguity and incomplete information
  6. Reflect on their own leadership instincts and identify potential blind spots in a transformation context
Notes for the ID: This is a decision-based simulation using two tracked variables: Transformation Momentum and Organisational Trust. All branching logic and consequence screens depend on these variables. Four learner profiles surface at the end based on variable combinations. Tone throughout should be case-study credible, not corporate. No bullet-pointed instruction screens. Every screen should feel like a real leadership situation, not a training module. Source of truth document: Architecting the AI-Ready Operating Model.

Branches
